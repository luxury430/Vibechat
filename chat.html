Your messages and personal data are secured. NeuroForge does not sell your data. You have full control over your online status, read receipts, and profile visibility through the settings panel.

🛡️3. Community Moderation

NeuroForge administrators and moderators reserve the right to restrict, mute, kick, or permanently ban accounts that violate these guidelines — with or without prior warning, at their sole discretion.

💬4. Channels & Content

Do not share illegal, NSFW, or harmful content in public channels. Channel owners are responsible for moderating their own spaces. Violations reported to admins will be actioned swiftly.

🌐5. Account Security

You are responsible for the security of your account. Never share your login credentials. Any suspicious activity must be reported to an admin immediately.

These terms may be updated at any time. Continued use of NeuroForge after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms. We will notify users of major changes via the in-app notification system.
